üìé Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Remote Sensing Artificial Intelligence Lab (RsAI_lab), College of Oceanography and Space Informatics, China University of Petroleum, advised by Prof. Genyun Sun and Associate Prof. Aizhu Zhang. At present, I am a Visiting Research Student within the Transparent Ocean team at the National Subsea Centre, Aberdeen, UK, working with Prof. Jinchang Ren. I obtained B.S. degree from the School of Geosciences, China University of Petroleum, China, in 2019.

My research interests are in hyperspectral remote sensing, especially:

  • Image processing: Feature extraction, classification, thin cloud and haze removal
  • Deep Learning: Weakly supervised learning, CNN and GAN
