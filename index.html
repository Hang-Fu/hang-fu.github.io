Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Remote Sensing Artificial Intelligence Lab (RsAI_lab), College of Oceanography and Space Informatics, China University of Petroleum, advised by Prof. Genyun Sun and Associate Prof. Aizhu Zhang and co-advised by Prof. Jinchang Ren in RGU. I will be going to Helmholtz-Zentrum Dresden-Rossendorf (HZDR), Germany as an exchange PhD student for 12 months, working with Prof. Pedram Ghamisi. I obtained B.S. degree from the School of Geosciences, China University of Petroleum, China, in 2019.

My research interests is in hyperspectral remote sensing, especially feature extraction and classification. I am currently working on thin cloud and haze removal from hyperspectral imagery.
